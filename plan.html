Break 90 Blueprint: 6-Week Strategy Plan

The 6-Week Plan is a structured system to build confidence, sharpen skills, and develop course strategy. Each week has a clear theme, guided club focus, and mindset objective — preparing you to break 90 with purpose, not luck.

  • Week 1: Contact & Accuracy – PW, 7i — Build a consistent strike pattern using face spray or foot powder.
  • Week 2: Distance Control – 8i, 9i, 54° — Practice three distances with clean setup and tempo.
  • Week 3: Mid-Iron Strategy – 6i, 3H — Emphasize large targets and GIR setups.
  • Week 4: Short Game Focus – GW, SW, Putter — Master basic chips, lags, and pressure drills.
  • Week 5: Pressure Reps – 4H, 5i, 3W — Simulate scoring drills and full-on course tactics.
  • Week 6: Challenge Round – Play, track, reflect, grow. Use best clubs by dispersion.
